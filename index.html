Privacy Policy

Slalom Hero — Pictus Studio — Last updated: April 27, 2026

In short: Slalom Hero does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to its own servers. The app has no backend. All your game data stays exclusively on your device.

1. Who We Are

Slalom Hero is developed by Pictus Studio. For any questions regarding this policy, you can reach us at: pictus.apps.studio@gmail.com

2. Data We Collect

Pictus Studio does not collect any personal data. The app has no server, no remote database, and does not transmit any information about you.

The following elements are stored locally on your device only:

  • Your best scores
  • Your preferences (sound, settings)

This data never leaves your phone and is deleted when you uninstall the app.

3. Advertising — Google AdMob

Slalom Hero displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. Google, as a third party, may collect and use certain data in order to serve relevant ads, including:

  • Your device's advertising identifier (Advertising ID)
  • Device information (model, operating system)
  • Ad interaction data
  • Approximate location (derived from IP address)

This data is collected and processed directly by Google, in accordance with Google's privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy

Google AdMob may use this information to personalize the ads shown in the app. You can reset or opt out of ad personalization at any time from your Android device settings (Settings → Privacy → Ads).

4. Internet Connection

The app uses the Internet solely to load and display advertisements. No other feature of the game requires a network connection.

5. Your Rights (GDPR)

Since Pictus Studio does not collect or process any personal data, we do not hold any information about you that could be accessed, modified, or deleted.

To exercise your rights regarding data collected by Google AdMob, please contact Google directly or refer to their privacy policy.

6. Children

Slalom Hero is not directed at children under the age of 13. The app does not collect any personal data, including from minors.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy if the app evolves (new features, changes in advertising providers, etc.). The date of the last update is shown at the top of this page. We encourage you to review it periodically.
